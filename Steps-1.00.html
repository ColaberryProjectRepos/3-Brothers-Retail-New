Problem Statement

The company 3 Brothers has been operating for two years, has shown consistent sales growth quarter after quarter, with a 77% increase in sales from 2019 to 2020. However, despite the increase in sales, the growth in profits has lagged behind, with only a 14% increase during the same period. While sales have grown from $564k in 2019 to over $1 million in 2020, profit has increased from $81k in 2019 to $93k in 2020. This discrepancy has resulted in a trend where the company's profit margin is shrinking as sales rise. As the company continues to increase sales, the decreasing profit margin presents a challenge that needs to be addressed to sustain profitability and ensure the long-term financial health of the business.
