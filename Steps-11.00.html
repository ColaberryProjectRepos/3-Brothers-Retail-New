Tweaking the Data

Before we make our calendar table or our measures table, we must clean this data up. This was brought over with European dates (day, month, year), but we need to make these American dates (month, day, year). To do this we will split them by delimitator and reconstruct them. Make sure to rename the delimitated columns so they are easier to keep up with. We will also change the type from number to text for ease of working with the data.
