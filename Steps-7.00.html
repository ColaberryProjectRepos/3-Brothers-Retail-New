Negative Insight

While sales have significantly increased, profits have not kept pace. 3 Brothers Retail experienced a 14% increase in profits between the first and second years of operation. This contrasts with the 77% increase in sales and has resulted in a reduced profit margin of -23% between the two years. 


This dashboard view for 2019 shows that we captured 82k in profit. Our highest quarter of sales was just under $200,000.


This dashboard view for 2020 shows we captured 93k in profit. The highest quarter in sales was nearly $400,000.
