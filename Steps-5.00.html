Problems Encountered

1. Dataset. Selecting the appropriate dataset was a challenging task. The project proceeded smoothly once a suitable dataset was identified.

2. Dates. The data was provided in a European date format and required conversion to US date format. This issue was resolved by separating the column and reconstructing it in a new custom column.

3. Blanks. Initially, the formula for the calendar table included one extra day, which affected the visuals and prevented them from displaying until the date range slider was adjusted.
